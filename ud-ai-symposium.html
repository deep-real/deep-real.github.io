UD AI Symposium 2023

Dates: September 25-26, 2023

Objectives

The UD AI Symposium will be held on Sept 25-26 at the University of Delaware. This 1.5-day event serves two purposes:

  • Bring all AI-relevant researchers at UD together and have a better understanding of our strengths.
  • Form up to three focused areas/teams for external funding opportunities.

More specifically, we are looking for teams, including external collaborators, to respond to the recent NSF AI Research Institute solicitations.
