For Prospective Students

We are ALWAYS looking for self-motivated PhD and visiting students.

Due to COVID-19, the Department of Computer and Information Sciences is waiving the GRE requirement, and not considering GRE results for our graduate program applicants this year for Spring 2021 and Fall 2021 applicants.

Machine learning research topics:
  • Robust machine learning: adversarial learning, few-shot learning, domain generalization, meta learning, OOD detection;
  • Explainable deep learning: Bayesian network, uncertainty assessment;
  • Spatial-temporal modeling: online/continue learning, GCNs, zero-shot abnormal detection;
Computer vision topics:
  • Human-centered vision: face/body/hand pose estimation, human-object interaction, human-operating-tool;
  • Vision based biomechanics: lifting/roofing/gait analysis, slip/fall evaluation;
We are looking for:
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent;
  • Excellent GPA records;
  • Excellent programming experience (>3 years) and proficient in Python or C/C++;
  • Relevant research experience (>1 years);
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
