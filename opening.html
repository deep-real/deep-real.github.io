For Prospective Students

We are ALWAYS looking for self-motivated PhD, visiting students, and Postdocs.

Strong candidates may be guaranteed with a 5-year TA or RA funding support once admitted. Due to COVID-19, the Department of Computer and Information Sciences is waiving the GRE requirement, and not considering GRE results for our graduate program applicants this year for Spring 2021 and Fall 2021 applicants.

Research Interests:

Deep transfer learning: meta-learning, domain generalization, few-short learning, cross-modal learning;

Adversarial training: data augmentation, data intervention, uncertainty assessment;

Graphic neural network: human pose estimation, human-object interaction, action prediction;

AI-based data analytics: biomechanics, bioinformatics, and geospatial data analysis.

We are looking for:

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent;

Excellent GPA records;

Excellent programming experience (>3 years) and proficient in Python or C/C++;

Relevant research experience (>1 years);

Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
