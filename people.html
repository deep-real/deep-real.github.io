Principal Investigator

Dr. Xi Peng

Dr. Xi Peng is an assistant professor of the Department of Computer and Information Sciences, a resident faculty of the Data Science Institute, an affiliate of Delaware Environmental Institute, all at the University of Delaware, Newark, DE, USA, where he is directing the Deep-REAL research group.

His current research interest is developing flexible, reliable, and explainable deep learning models, upon which scientific machine learning and human-centered AI can advance synergistically.

He received:

  • the B.E. degree from Beihang University, Beijing, China, in 2008;
  • the M.E. degree from Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, in 2011;
  • the Ph.D. degree from Rutgers University, NJ, USA, in 2018.

Publications: 

  • Total (until 6/15/2021): Conferences 33, Journals 10, Patents 2, Citations 1,513, h­-index 18, i10-­index 28.
  • Top conferences (20): CVPR 7, NeurIPS 3, ICLR 1, AAAI 1, ICCV 2, ECCV 3, IJCAI 1, KDD 1, NSDI 1.
  • Top journals (9): TPAMI 1, IJCV 3, TIP 1, THMS 1, JOB 2.
