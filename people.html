Principal Investigator

Dr. Xi Peng

Dr. Xi Peng is directing the Deep-REAL research group. Currently, he is holding three positions at UD:

  • Assistant Professor, Department of Computer and Information Sciences (CIS)
  • Resident Faculty, Data Science Institute (DSI)
  • Affiliate Faculty, of Delaware Environmental Institute (DENIN)

Dr. Peng works in the area of machine learning (ML), deep learning, and computer vision. His research interest is developing reliable, explainable, and efficient ML models, upon which scientific machine learning and human-centered AI can advance synergistically. His group is working in three research directions:

  • Robust and explainable ML: topology-aware robust and explainable optimization
  • Scientific ML: large and evolving data analysis in geography, hydrology, and coastal mapping
  • Human-centered AI: AI-based biomechanics analysis
